About Me

My name is Patrycja Gorczynkska. If you can't tell from my super long first and last name, I'm foregin. I am 100% polish. I was also born in South Africa! I know, crazy right?!

I'm for Chicago and moved to Arizona in October 2009. I spent my teens years in Fountain Hills. If you are not familiar with Fountain Hills, it's honestly just a bunch of old people. I graduated high school in 2013 and went to Scottsdale Community College and then transferred to ASU. I graduated ASU in May 2017 with a degree in Elementary and Special Education. I currently teach 1st/2nd grade Autism with Mesa Public Schools.

I really enjoy having a good time with the people I love. I like to hike, spend time with my cat, watch TV, and eat food. I am a BIG foodie. Some of my favorite foods are tacos, burgers, ice cream and chicken nuggets.
